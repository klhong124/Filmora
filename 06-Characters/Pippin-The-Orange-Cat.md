## Core Identity

Pippin is a tiny orange tabby cat with oversized round amber eyes, a white cream muzzle, a fluffy ringed tail, and a permanent look of cheerful confusion. He should always feel silly, cute, expressive, and harmless, like a small troublemaker who accidentally becomes the hero.

## Fixed Visual Anchors

- Face: round kitten-like face, small pink nose, cream-white muzzle, chubby cheeks, tiny lower fang sometimes visible.
- Age range: young adult cat with kitten-like proportions.
- Body: small compact body, plush fur, short legs, soft rounded paws.
- Hair: orange tabby fur with clear darker ginger stripes across forehead, cheeks, back, and tail.
- Eyes: very large round amber-gold eyes with bright catchlights.
- Skin: visible skin only on pink nose, paw pads, and inner ears.
- Silhouette: round head, tiny triangular ears, compact body, oversized fluffy ringed tail.
- Clothing: no full outfit; optional small teal neck scarf tied loosely on the left side.
- Props: tiny brass bell on the scarf when close-up detail is needed.
- Color palette: warm orange, ginger stripes, cream muzzle, pink nose, amber eyes, teal scarf accent.
- Recurring expression: wide-eyed silly grin or surprised "oops" face.

## Personality And Voice

- Personality: playful, chaotic, affectionate, easily distracted, accidentally brave.
- Desire: to chase interesting lights, snacks, and anything that jingles.
- Fear: deep water, vacuum-like noises, and being ignored.
- Speaking style: if voiced, short excited lines, curious meows, and comic pauses.
- Voice direction: high, soft, bouncy, cute, with mischievous timing.
