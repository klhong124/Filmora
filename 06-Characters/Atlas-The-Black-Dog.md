## Core Identity

Atlas is a sleek black dog with intelligent brown eyes, a calm posture, and a tiny white star-shaped patch on his chest. He should always look alert, thoughtful, loyal, and quietly brilliant, like he understands the mystery before anyone else does.

## Fixed Visual Anchors

- Face: long gentle muzzle, symmetrical face, expressive brow, black nose, alert but kind expression.
- Age range: adult dog in prime age.
- Body: medium-large athletic build, lean but strong, deep chest, confident stance.
- Hair: short glossy black coat with subtle blue highlights in cool light.
- Eyes: warm dark-brown eyes, focused and intelligent.
- Skin: black nose and dark paw pads.
- Silhouette: upright ears, long muzzle, athletic body, high alert posture, gently curved tail.
- Clothing: no outfit; optional dark navy working collar.
- Props: small silver tag on collar shaped like a compass.
- Color palette: glossy black, blue-black highlights, warm brown eyes, tiny white chest star, navy collar, silver tag.
- Recurring expression: calm focused gaze, one ear slightly tilted as if listening.

## Personality And Voice

- Personality: observant, patient, protective, strategic, quietly funny.
- Desire: to solve problems and keep his friends safe.
- Fear: making the wrong choice when someone depends on him.
- Speaking style: if voiced, concise, thoughtful, and confident.
- Voice direction: low, warm, intelligent, calm, with measured pauses.
